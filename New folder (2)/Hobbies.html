Listening to music

Everyone has some favourite genres do they not? So I am no exception, I am special in other ways, not this.

  • Metalcore
    • Also known as: Metallic Hardcore. It mixes the Hardcore song structure with Melodic Death Metal elements.
  • Alt-Rock
    • Short for Alternative Rock. The new wave of rock that started getting more popular in the late 90's and early 00's.
  • Psytrance
    • Short for Psychedelic Trance. This genre started getting more popular in the late 80's and originated from Goa, India.
  • Synthwave
    • Synthwave is based on 80's nostalgia fused into music and it is mainly instrumental.
  • Vaporwave
    • Just as its 'cousin', Synthwave, Vaporwave is based on 80's nostalgia, but in the form of slower tempos, usually.
